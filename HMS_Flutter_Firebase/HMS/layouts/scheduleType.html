The Doctors Panel in the Healthcare Management System (HMS) is a core feature designed to provide seamless interaction between patients and healthcare professionals.
It includes a diverse team of medical experts from various specialties such as Medicine, Psychiatry, Cardiology, Gynecology, and Nephrology, ensuring comprehensive and personalized care for patients.
Through the system, patients can book appointments, consult doctors remotely, and access their medical records. This panel enables real-time collaboration among specialists to offer accurate diagnoses, treatment plans, and follow-up care, promoting efficient healthcare management with a user-centric approach.
